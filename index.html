OUR SERVICES

We provide the best service to farmers.

AI Based Crop Disease Classification & Suggestions From Agri-experts.

Rural farmers of Bangladesh don't get access to Agri-experts when their crops are suffering from diseases, which causes them a huge loss in their crop production. Once farmers get registered to FarmersZone, they'll be able to classify their crop diseases with a single image and get experts advisory. Our soil testing and crop disease classification service will help them to increase their crop production upto 20%.

Cost Reduction

Our farmers buy low quality Agri-inputes like Seeds, Fartilizers, Pesticides etc from local vendors at a high price. These low quality Agri-inputes lows down their crop production and they face huge financial loss. Through our platfrom, farmers can buy quality agri-inputs directly from menufacturing companies and this'll save their cost upto 15%.

Direct Buyers With Best Price, No Middleman

Farmers of Bangladesh don't get proper price for their crops because of middle men and supply chain bottlenecks. For example, few months back we saw in nwes that Chili's price were only 2 TK per kilogram in Nilphamari and at the same time it was 35-40 TK per kilogram in Dhaka. This happens only because of the supply chain bottlenecks and we aim to solve this. Farmers will be able to sell their crops directly to bulk buyers and industries through our platform, which will help them to get a better price of their crops upto 20%.

Exporting in Middle East and Europe

We have plans to exports fruits & vegetables in Middle East and European countries in future. There are huge open market out there.

ABOUT US

We are Farmers Zone.

The total size of our Agri-market is more than $4.5 billion. But unfortunately, every year more than 7 million tones of rice is damaged completely due to various diseases and low quality Agri-inputs in cultivation. 15-20% of the total Agri production is damaged every year in our country because our rural farmers can't get quality Agri-inputs and access of Agri-Experts and their suggestions in rural areas, which causes a huge damage to our farmers and economy. On the other hand, 70-75% of our farmers don't get proper price for their produces due to supply chain bottlenecks and middlemen.

To solve all these problem for our farmers, we came forward to a develop a platform named "FarmersZone" with cutting-edge technologies like AI, Deep learning, Computer Vision which will provide 360-degree solutions to the farmers.
Once farmers get registered to FarmersZone, they'll be able to get training, buy quality Agri-inputs (seeds, fertilizers, pesticides etc.) directly from companies and this will save their cost up to 15%. By using our Artificial Intelligence based services, they'll be able to classify crop diseases with a single image and get experts advisory. Our soil testing and crop disease classification service will help them to increase their crop production up to 20%. Farmers will be able to sell their crops directly to bulk buyers and industries through our platform, which will help them to get a better price of their crops up to 20%. This is how farmers can increase their profit up to 50% by using our platform. Our data driven demand forecasting will help farmers to analyze the market in advance. We also aim to export vegetables and fruits in the Middle East and European market in future, which'll be the most exciting part.

To enable this AI based services we're working on one of our Scopus Index Research Paper titled "Classifying the Usual Leaf Diseases of Paddy Plants in Bangladesh Using Multilayered CNN Architecture" Paper Link. In this research we worked on different types of Paddy plant diseases. We also have successfully completed our research on some of Potato and Tomato diseases and continuing many other crop diseases. Hopefully we'll introduce this services to our farmers very soon.
